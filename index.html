Project Summary

We are going to conduct a short survey to understand students' awareness of mental health, followed by an interactive workshop focusing on stress management, emotional vocabulary, and safe help-seeking. Utkarsha and Shital will lead the facilitation, while Revati and Piyush will handle documentation and website development.

Workshop Plan (Short)
  1. Icebreaker: 5 min — name + one feeling (happy/sad/excited)
  2. Mini-talk: 8 min — what is mental health (simple terms)
  3. Activity 1: Feeling Wheel (20 min) — group matching
  4. Activity 2: Stress-buster stations (20 min) — breathing, sketching, stretching
  5. Reflection & Safe-Help: 10 min — who to talk to and how
  6. Feedback + Photo & Certificates (10 min)
